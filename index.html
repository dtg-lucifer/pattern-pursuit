Predictability

Ever wondered how predictable you are? What if I told you I can anticipate your behavior based on your past choices? Let's make it challenging for me to predict you. Choose between heads or tails, and I'll attempt to guess your decision. The more you vary your choices, the harder it becomes for me to decipher your pattern. PatternPursuit
Can you prove yourself to be truly unpredictable?
The rules are simple: you'll be presented with a choice between "left" or "right". My task is to guess your selection before it's revealed. If I guess right, I take some money from you. But if I guess wrong, you'll get money from me. The key is to outsmart me by being as random as possible, which will help you earn more money.
